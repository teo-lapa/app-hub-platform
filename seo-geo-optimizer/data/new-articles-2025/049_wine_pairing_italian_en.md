## Fundamental Pairing Rules
**Concordance**: Wine and dish same intensity
**Contrast**: Balance opposite flavors (sweet-acid, fat-tannin)
**Territoriality**: "What grows together goes together"

## Classic Pairings
**Antipasti**: Prosecco, Vermentino, Pinot Grigio
**Primi**: Carbonara→Frascati, Ragù→Sangiovese, Pesto→Vermentino
**Secondi Meat**: Bistecca→Brunello, Ossobuco→Barolo
**Secondi Fish**: Sea Bass→Vermentino, Fritto→Franciacorta
**Cheeses**: Fresh→Verdicchio, Aged→Brunello, Blue→Passito
**Desserts**: Tiramisù→Moscato, Cantuccini→Vin Santo

## Restaurant Wine List
**Size**: Trattoria 20-35, Restaurant 40-80, Fine Dining 80-200 labels
**Distribution**: White 40%, Red 45%, Rosé 5%, Sparkling 10%
**Pricing**: Entry 40%, Mid-range 40%, Premium 15%, Icon 5%
**By Glass**: 8-12 options (3 white, 3 red, 2 sparkling, 1-2 rosé)

## Strategic Pricing
**Markup**: Entry 2.8-3.2x, Mid 2.5-3.0x, Premium 2.2-2.8x, Icon 2.0-2.5x
**Example**: Vermentino cost 12 CHF → sale 36-38 CHF

## Staff Training
**Basic (all servers)**: 4-6 hours initial + quarterly refresh
**Sommelier**: WSET Level 2 (800-1'200 CHF) or AIS (1'500-2'500 CHF)

**Tastings**: Monthly events 35-50 CHF, participants become regular customers
