# Staff Selection and Training Restaurant: Complete Guide

## Meta Description
Complete guide staff selection and training restaurant: effective recruitment, onboarding, continuous training, retention and team management to reduce turnover by 40%.

## Keywords
restaurant staff selection, catering personnel training, restaurant recruitment, reduce turnover

---

## Introduction
Qualified motivated staff is restaurant's most valuable asset. Average catering turnover Switzerland 40-60%/year. Effective strategies reduce to 15-25%.

## Recruitment
**Channels**: Job.ch, Gastrojobs.ch, referrals (best source), hotel schools, social media
**Process**: CV screening → Phone interview → In-person interview → Practical test → References
**Time**: 7-14 days

## Onboarding
**Week 1**: Tour, team introduction, documentation, shadowing, first supervised tasks
**Month 1**: Weekly check-ins, buddy system, training modules, 30-day evaluation

## Continuous Training
**Technical**: Preparation techniques, recipe standardization, portion control, HACCP
**Soft Skills**: Communication, teamwork, stress management
**Budget**: 1'000-2'000 CHF/employee/year

## Turnover Management
**Main causes**: Non-competitive salaries (35%), unsustainable hours (25%), lack of growth (15%)
**Retention strategies**: Competitive compensation, work-life balance, career paths, positive culture, benefits

## Shift Planning
**Software**: 7shifts (30-80 CHF/month), When I Work, Planday
**Best Practices**: Publish 2 weeks ahead, allow swaps between colleagues, respect preferences

## Performance Evaluation
**Quarterly reviews**: Work quality, productivity, teamwork, customer service, attendance
**Scale 1-5**, specific feedback, development plan

## KPIs
Retention >75%, Turnover <25%, Average tenure >18 months, Training >20 hours/year

## Conclusion
Investment in people management generates ROI 300-500% in 2 years. Turnover reduction 40-60% → 15-25%, Customer satisfaction +20-30%.
