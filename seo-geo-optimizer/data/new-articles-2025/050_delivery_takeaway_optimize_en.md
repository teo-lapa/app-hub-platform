## Introduction
Delivery and take-away represent 25-40% modern restaurant revenue. Optimization increases margins 20-35% and customer satisfaction +30%.

## Swiss Delivery Platforms
**Uber Eats**: 25-35% commission, maximum coverage
**Just Eat**: 13-15%, annual contract
**Eat.ch**: 12-18%, strong German Switzerland
**Own System**: 0% commission, full control, 3'000-8'000 CHF development

**Optimal mix**: Own website 40% + 2 platforms 60%

## Delivery Menu
**Ideal dishes**: Pizza, dry pasta, burgers, paninis, salads (dressing separate)
**Avoid**: Fried foods, complex assembly
**Pricing**: 10-15% higher than dine-in (compensate commissions)

## Professional Packaging
**Pizza**: Micro-perforated cardboard, 0.40-0.80 CHF
**Pasta**: Aluminum container, 0.60-1.20 CHF
**Burgers**: Cardboard box, 0.50-0.90 CHF
**Total per order**: 1.50-3.00 CHF

**Branding**: Logo, QR-code, social media â†’ +25% repeat orders
**Eco**: Recycled cardboard, bioplastic (+15-25% costs, high marketing value)

## Operational Management
**Dedicated zone**: Separate kitchen area for delivery
**Ideal preparation time**: 15-20 min
**Order management software**: Deliverect (150-300 CHF/month)
**Quality control**: Pre-delivery checklist, Mystery Orders 2-4x/month

## Delivery
**Options**: Platform couriers (high commission), third-party (4-7 CHF fixed), own (only if >100 deliveries/day)
**Zone**: 3-5 km radius (delivery <30 min)

## Marketing
**Promotions**: First order -20%, weak days -15%, large orders -10%
**Reviews**: >4.5/5 stars target, respond all reviews within 24h

## KPIs
**Operational**: Preparation time 15-20 min, delivery time <35 min, error rate <2%
**Financial**: Delivery revenue 25-40% total, average order 35-50 CHF, margin 15-25%
**Customers**: Rating >4.5/5, repeat rate >35%

## Zurich Pizzeria Case Study
**Before**: 60% Uber Eats, margin 8%
**After**: 40% own website, optimized menu, margin 22%, revenue +45%
